Spirasterize

A website for image spiral-rasterization.

Drag & Drop images in the Display section to Spirasterize them.

The image on load is a picture of Mona Lisa and an example of what Spirasterize does.

Hop on to the Display section and try it out.

NOTE- The images may take time to load, wait for it to load completely to download it.
You can drop images anywhere in the screen.
